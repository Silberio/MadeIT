- ABOUT US -

"Leadership and learning are indispensable to each other."

A dedicated group of committed, highly-educated, non-corrupt entrepreneurs with a passion for deisgn, user accesibility and teaching, for whom no challenge is ever too difficult. Based in Stockholm, Sweden, MadeIT started off as small project in which great minds with each a technical area of expertise in programming, graphics, teaching and sweet-talking, work together to deliver agencies and businesses all around the world, a tailored, pedagogic and complete package of online courses, for stream or download, as well as any education-related content for buisness and government. MadeIT provides a variety of packages to suit your needs, from thebasic online course with interactive video and reading material to a complete package with hand-picked educators. Need an educational presentation for a seminar? Need quick course on workspace safety? Maybe you need a webpage and server for storing tests?
